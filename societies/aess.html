About IEEE AESS

rocket_launch

The IEEE Aerospace and Electronic Systems Society (AESS) is dedicated to advancing the science and engineering of aerospace and electronic systems. AESS covers a broad spectrum of technologies including radar, navigation, avionics, space systems, and unmanned aerial vehicles.

At IEEE RVU Student Branch, the AESS chapter focuses on exploring cutting-edge aerospace technologies, electronic systems design, and their applications through workshops, technical talks, project collaborations, and competitions.
