Welcome to RAISQE'26

Welcome to the International Conference on Responsible AI, Secure System, and Quantum Engineering (RAISQE 2026). This global forum brings together researchers, academicians, industry experts, and practitioners to explore groundbreaking advancements at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Cybersecurity, and Quantum Technologies.
RAISQE 2026 aims to foster dialogue on building responsible AI frameworks, designing secure and resilient systems, and harnessing the transformative power of quantum engineering. Through keynote talks, technical sessions, workshops, and panel discussions, the conference provides a unique platform for knowledge exchange, collaboration, and innovation.
